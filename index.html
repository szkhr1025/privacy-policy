Privacy Policy General

Last Updated: 2026-02-15

This Privacy Policy explains how our applications (the "App") handle information. This page is intended to be a general privacy policy that can be used across multiple apps provided by us, unless an app presents a separate policy.

1. Information We Collect

By default, we do not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers.

  • Data processed locally: Some features may access information on your device (e.g., browser tabs, clipboard content, files, or settings) only to provide core functionality. Unless otherwise stated, such information is processed on your device and is not sent to us.
  • Settings: The App may store your preferences locally on your device (for example, using browser/device storage).

2. Permissions

Some apps may request permissions (for example, access to tabs, storage, or network) to perform the features you explicitly use. We use these permissions only for the intended functionality and do not use them for tracking.

3. Third-Party Services

Unless the App explicitly indicates otherwise, we do not share your information with third parties and do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

4. Data Retention

Any data stored by the App is generally retained on your device. You can typically remove it by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's local storage.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: mail@hiroyukisuzuki.com.
